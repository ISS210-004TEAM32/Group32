What is AI?

"Artificial intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems" (TechTarget).

In simple terms, the new Snapchat AI or other Artifical Intelligence produces computer written responses immediately based on the context of your message. For example, saying "send me a cookie recipe," the Artificial Intelligence will pick up your question and immediately send a step-by-step cookie recipe.

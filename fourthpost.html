Awareness Online

In this section we will be diving into the awareness users should have when exploring the internet and using social media.

Posted by Jonathan Abraskin on April 28, 2023

Welcome to this page, it is dedicated to raising awareness about cybersecurity regarding children. With the increasing reliance on technology in our daily lives, it's more important than ever to understand the risks and threats that come with being connected to the internet. Cyber attacks are becoming more sophisticated and widespread, and it's crucial that individuals and organizations take proactive steps to protect themselves and their sensitive information.

Here we provide information and resources to help you stay safe online. We cover a range of topics, from basic cybersecurity tips for beginners to more advanced techniques for those who want to enhance their digital security. Our goal is to empower you with the knowledge and tools you need to protect yourself, your children, and your online assets from cyber threats.

We believe that cybersecurity is a shared responsibility, and everyone has a role to play in creating a safer online environment. Whether you're a student, a business owner, or simply a concerned individual, our website has something for you. Explore this page to learn more about cybersecurity and how your family can stay safe online.

How to increase you and your child's cyber awareness

With the recent growth in technology, children are now much more frequent users of the internet. While this isn't necesarily a bad thing, it does raise a few questions, How can I make sure my child doesn't see anything I don't want them to? or How can I supervise my child while they're online?

To answer these questions first assess what your child is using their device for, whether it be games, school, etc. from there most devices should have some sort of parental controls that can limit the amount of screen time and applications the child can use.

From there, certain providers allow you to track and view what your child is viewing. you can also put on parental restrictions

Other tips

Other ways your family can stay safe online are to discuss general rules about what people can do online. If you let you child online please teach them about phising. Phising is the act of someone trying to get your infromation by posing as someone else or another company. by increasing your childs awareness about phising, it'll make them a lot less likely to fall for it.

Cybersecurity is everywhere and there to protect you and your information.

Without cybersecurity, all of your data would be extremely easy to get and use. These comapnies work day in and out to protect your information
